# 🗳️ Decoding the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election

### A Data-Driven Analysis of Electoral Shifts, Vote Share Dynamics & Regional Dominance

This project was developed as part of the **Codebasics Resume Project Challenge**. The objective was to analyze the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election using SQL, Power BI, Excel, and Data Storytelling to uncover meaningful insights from election data.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The analysis focuses on understanding:

* Political shifts between 2021 and 2026
* Party vote share distribution
* Region-wise party dominance
* Voter turnout patterns
* Top-performing candidates
* Closest election contests
* Largest victory margins

The project combines SQL-based analysis with Power BI visualizations and presentation storytelling to communicate insights effectively.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Political Swing Analysis

* TVK emerged as the largest party with **108 seats**.
* TVK recorded the biggest political gain between 2021 and 2026.
* DMK experienced the largest decline in seat count.

### Regional Dominance

* TVK dominated Chennai Metro, Kongu, and South Tamil Nadu.
* AIADMK led in the Central region.
* DMK maintained strong performance in the Delta region.

### Candidate Performance

* ECR P Saravanan recorded the highest vote count.
* 9 out of the Top 10 highest vote-getting candidates belonged to TVK.

### Election Competitiveness

* The closest contest was decided by just **1 vote**.
* The largest victory margin exceeded **98,000 votes**.
